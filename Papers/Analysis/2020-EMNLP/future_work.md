# Future Work

## Short-Term Improvements

- Explore additional knowledge bases to improve commonsense inference.
- Improve the parallel corpus creation pipeline to reduce noise in weakly labeled similes.
- Refine the literal-to-simile transformation process for better grammaticality and contextual fit.

## Long-Term Research Directions

- Extend the style-transfer framework to other creative NLG tasks.
- Apply the same approach to pun generation, sarcasm generation, and hyperbole generation, as suggested in the paper.
- Investigate stronger models or decoding methods for more controllable creative generation.

## Potential Extensions

- Adapt the method to other figurative language phenomena beyond similes.
- Combine commonsense reasoning with richer discourse context so generated figures of speech fit longer contexts better.
- Explore broader human-centered evaluation settings for creative writing assistance.
