Problem
1)No prior simile generation tool almost other researches are based on imile classification and identification
2)Lack of parallel training data for simile generation: there was no large scale parallelcorpus of pair like (literal sentence, simile) pair.
3)Earlier metaphor generation methods relied on templates,heurisitcs or lexical databases. Those do not preserve contextual core meaning.
4)Difficulty in identifing real or implicit meaning of simile
5)there is no creative natural langauage generation framework 

Existing work
1)Prior computational work has focused mainly on simile detection and analysis rather than generation.
2)Simile detection studies include annotation and recognition of similes in user-generated and literary text.
3)Earlier metaphor generation methods relied on templates, heuristics, lexical databases, or word embeddings.
4)More recent metaphor generation work used neural models and seq2seq masking approaches.
